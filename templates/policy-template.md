## Authoring Instructions

The following instructions apply when creating a new policy document using this template.

**Formatting:**
- Use numbered sections: 1, 1.1, 1.2.
- Use short, direct sentences.
- One rule or statement per numbered item.

**Language:**
- Use: "must", "must not", "only", "does not".
- Do not use: "should" (unless explicitly required by the subject matter), "generally", "typically".
- Do not use narrative tone.
- Do not use legal fluff or redundant phrasing.

**Content:**
- Do not invent policy.
- Do not reinterpret meaning.
- Do not simplify logic.
- Do not merge decision branches.
- Do not include process, workflow, or procedures in a definitions document.
- Do not include definitions work in a process document.

**Classification Logic (Locked):**
- Modern work ≠ folklore.
- Cultural influence ≠ folklore.
- Folklore = collective origin + no identifiable author.
- Modern retelling = modern work.
- Do not alter this logic.

**Decision States (Locked):**
Only the following decision states are permitted: Approved, Approved with Limits, Held, Restricted, Rejected, Escalated.
Do not introduce additional states.
